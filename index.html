online therapy in Toronto & the rest of Ontario.

My approach is insight-oriented, relational, and existential. In session, I’m engaged and curious. I don’t just sit back and listen - I reflect what I see, gently ask hard questions, and stay with you as things unfold.

I draw on psychodynamic, emotion-focused, attachment, and EMDR approaches to explore how past experiences - especially those shaped by difference, shame, or family dynamics - still echo in the present. I also integrate Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) and Acceptance Commitment Therapy (ACT) to help you notice unhelpful narratives, relate to them more flexibly, and take action towards change.

frequently asked questions

I only offer online therapy and counselling via videoconferencing.

The total fee for a 50-minute session of individual psychotherapy is $160. Fees are payable at the end of each therapy session by a credit card that is kept on file.

Every insurance plan is different in terms of coverage. Clients should verify with their insurance provider whether psychotherapy services offered online/virtually by a Registered Psychotherapist are covered and the level of coverage for such services.

Please note, I do not offer direct billing. At the end of each session, however, I provide a receipt that you can submit to your insurance provider for reimbursement.

The course of therapy will vary from person to person. Some prefer to come on a weekly or biweekly basis for supportive therapy and ongoing processing. Some may be looking to work within a fixed timeframe on specific goals. And others may start off on a weekly basis to tend to pressing circumstances and then switch frequency. We can certainly collaborate to figure out what suits you best.

I highly recommend scheduling a 15-minute consult prior to booking a full intake session as it offers an opportunity to start exploring fit, guide you through next steps, and go over administrative matters.
